Dysfunctional chess prodigy Noel (Alia Shawkat) wants to impress their mom with a thriving indoor spearmint garden. Reaching for the last Non-Dairy Mint Chip Bon-Bons, they meet Stephen (Timothee Chalamet), a ghost hunter trying to invent a mask that will protect phantoms from COVID-19. Socially distanced and never seeing the bottom half of the other's face, they discover the true meaning of the holiday season.
